Award Winning Actors

Hiroyuki Sanada

Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series (2024) Recognised for his critically acclaimed performance as Lord Toranaga in the hit historical TV series Shōgun.

Noah Wyle

Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series (2025) Praised for his leading role in the medical drama The Pitt, which also won Outstanding Drama Series.

Stephen Graham

BAFTA TV Award for Leading Actor (2026) Won major praise for his performance in the Netflix limited series Adolescence, one of the biggest TV award winners of the year.

People’s Favourite Actors

Pedro Pascal

One of the internet’s most popular TV actors thanks to major roles in The Last of Us, Narcos, and The Mandalorian. Widely praised by fans for charisma and emotional performances.

Jenna Ortega

Massively popular with younger audiences after starring in Netflix’s Wednesday, becoming one of streaming television’s breakout stars.

Adam Scott

Gained huge popularity from the psychological thriller series Severance, which became one of the most talked-about modern TV dramas.
