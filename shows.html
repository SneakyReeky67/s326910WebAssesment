Trending TV Shows

The Last of Us

A hugely popular post-apocalyptic drama praised for its emotional storytelling, cinematic visuals, and faithful adaptation of the video game franchise. The series continues to dominate streaming charts and online discussion.

Severance

A psychological sci-fi thriller centred around workers whose memories are surgically split between work and personal life. Known for its unique concept, unsettling atmosphere, and award-level performances.

The Bear

A fast-paced drama following a chef trying to rebuild his family restaurant in Chicago. Viewers love its intense kitchen realism, emotional depth, and standout acting performances.

Award Winners

Shogun

One of the biggest recent award winners, taking multiple major television awards including Outstanding Drama Series at the Emmys. Praised for its cinematography, historical storytelling, costumes, and performances.

Baby Reindeer

Netflix’s psychological drama became one of the biggest winners at the 2024 Emmy Awards, taking home Outstanding Limited Series alongside awards for writing and acting performances.

Succession

A modern television powerhouse that won numerous Emmy and Golden Globe awards, especially for Outstanding Drama Series and acting performances. Famous for its sharp dialogue, corporate family conflicts, and dark humour.
